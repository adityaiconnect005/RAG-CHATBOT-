HDFC Focused Fund Direct Growth

HDFC Focused Fund Direct Growth is a Equity Mutual Fund Scheme launched by HDFC Mutual Fund. This scheme was made available to investors on 10 Dec 1999. Dhruv Muchhal is the Current Fund Manager of HDFC Focused Fund Direct Growth fund. The fund currently has an Asset Under Management(AUM) of ₹9,37,048 Cr and the Latest NAV as of 10 Jun 2026 is ₹251.02.
